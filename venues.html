Recommended Venues

Check out some of our favorite venues for ideas on where to host your next event!
If you have any questions or would like some assistance in booking, please contact us for more details.

Lake Worth Casino

10 S Ocean Blvd, Lake Worth, FL
(561) 533-7395

A unique 3,000 square foot ballroom that offers breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean with wrap-around terraces and space for up to 250 guests. It provides a perfect location for weddings, special occasions and other events.

The Boynton Beach Woman's Club

1010 S Federal Hwy, Boynton Beach, FL
(561) 369-2300

A National Historic Place with multiple spaces available for events that offers high, wood vaulted ceilings and space for up to 200 guests. It provides a beautiful setting for many types of events.

Intracoastal Park Clubhouse

2240 N Federal Hwy, Boynton Beach, FL
(561) 742-6650

A waterfront clubhouse with a second story wrap around deck overlooking the Intracoastal Waterway and space for up to 50 guests. It is great for meetings, parties, showers and intimate weddings.

Old School Square

51 N Swinton Ave, Delray Beach, FL
(561) 243-7922

Beautifully restored spaces provide a unique, historic setting for any occasion. From small seminars to large receptions, it offers a flexable setting for events.

Morikami Museum and Japanese Gardens

4000 Morikami Park Rd, Delray Beach, FL
(561) 495-0233

Morikami provides a distinctive venue of gardens featuring several Japanese bridges, waterfalls, and other beautifully landscaped areas. It can accommodate an array of social events with space for up to 150 guests.
